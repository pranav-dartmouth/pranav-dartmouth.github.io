My Academic Interests

Academically, I have a number of broad and overlapping interests. I've had some preliminary exposure to geography, political science, and linguistics through my time at college and I definitely hope to do something at the intersection of these fields. But I've also had some very valuable experiences interning for an health research initiative and participating in environmental activism, so I'm far from settled in terms of what I would actually like to do in my career. Regardless of what I end up choosing, I aspire to do something interdisciplinary and something that will have an impact towards an issue I am passionate about.
