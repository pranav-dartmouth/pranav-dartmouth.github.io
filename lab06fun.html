What I Like to Do for Fun

I enjoy reading, cooking, and traveling when I'm not doing anything else.

Reading

Reading for pleasure is something you'll always find me doing when I have even a little bit of spare time. I thoroughly enjoy reading both fiction and nonfiction, but my personal preference is nonfiction.

Cooking

Cooking has never been something I've been great at, to be completely frank. But I've always enjoyed cooking whatever simple things I can because my family appreciates it and it helps me destress a little bit.
